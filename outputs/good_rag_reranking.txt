
Q: What coffee-to-water ratio and water temperature should I use for a strong pour-over?
[retrieved: docs/ratios.md#0 (8), docs/brewing_guide.md#0 (7), docs/temperature.md#0 (5)]
A: For a strong pour-over, you should use a coffee-to-water ratio of 1:15 and water temperature between 195°F and 205°F.

Q: What kind of water should I use for brewing?
[retrieved: docs/brewing_guide.md#3 (10), docs/temperature.md#0 (2), docs/brewing_guide.md#1 (0)]
A: You should use filtered water with moderate mineral content, around 150 ppm total dissolved solids.
